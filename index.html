Our Message

"Let's unite to protect our rivers! They are the lifeblood of our planet, providing water, food, and habitats for countless species. Yet, they face numerous threats from pollution, over-extraction, and climate change. Every small action counts, and collective efforts can have a significant impact. Let's work together to preserve the beauty, health, and vitality of our rivers for future generations."

What We Do

As a River Care NGO, our mission is to protect and preserve rivers and their surrounding ecosystems. We focus on:

Protecting rivers from pollution, over-extraction, and habitat destruction.

üìö Education and Awareness

ü§ù Community Engagement

üî¨ Research and Monitoring

Conducting research and monitoring to understand river health and develop effective conservation strategies.

Key Activities:

  • Organizing river cleanups and promoting community involvement
  • Monitoring water quality to identify sources of pollution
  • Restoring riparian habitats and wetlands to support biodiversity
  • Developing educational programs and materials to raise awareness
  • Advocating for policy changes that protect rivers and their ecosystems
