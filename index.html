Our Vision

🎯

Pollution Control

To work toward all kind of river pollution control and purify and treat all kinds of river pollutants with technology and human efforts so that unpolluted river water is available for domestic, commercial and industrial consumption.

🐟

Ecosystem Restoration

To help in restoration of aging aquatic ecosystem (Eutrophication) which is disturbed due to over pollution of Rivers.

Our Actions

What We Do

As a River Care NGO, our mission is to protect and preserve rivers and their surrounding ecosystems. We focus on:

🛡️

River Conservation

Protecting rivers from pollution, over-extraction, and habitat destruction.

📚

Education & Awareness

Educating local communities, schools, and stakeholders about river conservation.

🤝

Community Engagement

Collaborating with local communities to promote sustainable practices.

🔬

Research & Monitoring

Conducting research and monitoring to understand river health and develop effective conservation strategies.

Key Activities

  • Organizing river cleanups and promoting community involvement
  • Monitoring water quality to identify sources of pollution
  • Restoring riparian habitats and wetlands to support biodiversity
  • Developing educational programs and materials to raise awareness
  • Advocating for policy changes that protect rivers and their ecosystems
